Garden Tools

Garden tools are a set of instruments or equipment that are used to cultivate, maintain, and improve gardens, lawns, and other outdoor spaces. These tools come in a wide variety of shapes and sizes, each designed for specific tasks such as digging, planting, weeding, pruning, and watering.

Soils & Fertilizers

Soil is the foundation of any garden or plant growth, and it is important to understand its composition and characteristics. Soil is made up of various components such as sand, silt, clay, organic matter, and minerals. Fertilizers are materials used to enrich soil and provide nutrients that plants need to grow.

Footwear & Clothing

Gardening clothing and footwear protect gardeners while working outdoors. Items include a wide-brimmed hat, durable gloves, long-sleeved shirts and pants, aprons, and rubber boots. By wearing proper gear, gardeners can work safely and comfortably in their gardens.
